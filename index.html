About Me

I am Lei Lei (雷磊), currently a Ph.D. candidate at Purple Mountain Observatory (PMO) and University of Science and Technology of China (USTC).

I was born and raised in two villages in Gansu (陇中) Province in western China, called BuDong (堡东, 天水) Village and CiWa (茨屲, 定西) Village, where there are brilliant starry skies, rolling mountains, shallow dams, and slender rivers.

Contact: leilei@pmo.ac.cn

Family: My wife is Dr. Qiao Li (李巧), a postdoc Solar Physicist at PMO.
